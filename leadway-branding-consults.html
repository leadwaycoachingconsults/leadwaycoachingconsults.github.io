About Coach Victoria

I Know What It Feels Like to Work Hard and Still Feel Invisible

There was a time when I had the skills, the drive, and the passion, but the money just was not matching my effort. I was working hard, showing up every day, but the world did not know I existed. My brand was silent. And a silent brand, as I now teach thousands of people, does not pay bills.

That season of struggle became my strongest teacher. It pushed me to understand branding not as a luxury for big corporations, but as a survival tool for every entrepreneur who wants to be taken seriously and paid accordingly.

Today, I am the founder of Leadway Branding Consults and Leadway Coaching Consults, where I have trained over 910 professionals across Nigeria and Africa to build personal brands that attract clients, command respect, and generate consistent income.

When AI arrived and changed the game, I did not just adapt. I became the person who teaches others how to use it. Through my SMART AI Digital Skills brand, I have helped home-based entrepreneurs, nursing mothers, and small business owners understand that AI is not a threat to their livelihood. It is the shortcut they have been waiting for.

I am also a published author with books including Personal Branding Blueprint and Implementation Pills, a TEDx speaker, and a woman who believes deeply that financial independence is the foundation of confidence for every woman in Africa.

If you are ready to stop being the best-kept secret in your industry and start building a brand that speaks, sells, and scales, you are in the right place.
